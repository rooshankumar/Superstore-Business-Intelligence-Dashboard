# Superstore Business Intelligence Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales, profitability, customer segments, and product performance using the Superstore dataset. The dashboard was developed in Power BI to provide business stakeholders with actionable insights into revenue generation, profit trends, regional performance, and product profitability.

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* DAX
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization

## Dataset

The dataset contains retail transaction records including:

* Sales
* Profit
* Quantity
* Discount
* Customer Information
* Product Information
* Geographic Information

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Performance Overview

Key metrics:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin
* Total Orders
* Total Quantity
* Average Discount

Business insights:

* Technology generated the highest share of revenue.
* The business achieved over $2.3M in sales and $286K in profit.
* Profitability improved steadily between 2014 and 2017.

### 2. Regional Profitability Analysis

Focus areas:

* State-level sales and profit distribution
* Regional profitability comparison
* Customer segment profitability

Business insights:

* West region generated the highest sales and profit.
* Central region showed weaker profit performance despite strong sales.
* Profitability varied significantly across states.

### 3. Product Analysis

Focus areas:

* Category performance
* Sub-category sales ranking
* Profitability versus revenue analysis
* Category profit trends

Business insights:

* Technology contributed the largest share of sales.
* Phones were the highest revenue-generating sub-category.
* Several sub-categories generated high revenue but comparatively lower profit.
